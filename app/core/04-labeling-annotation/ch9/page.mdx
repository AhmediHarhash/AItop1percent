# Chapter 9 — Labeling Economics and Program Management

Labeling costs money — often more than teams expect. The true cost includes not just annotator time, but tooling, quality control, rework, management overhead, and the opportunity cost of slow iteration. This chapter covers the economics of labeling and the program management discipline that keeps operations sustainable at scale.
