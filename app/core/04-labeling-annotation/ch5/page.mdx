# Chapter 5 — Annotator Wellbeing and Harmful Content Handling

Labeling operations inevitably expose annotators to harmful content — violence, abuse, self-harm, hate speech, and other material that takes a real psychological toll. In 2026, annotator wellbeing is not a nice-to-have. It is an operational requirement that affects label quality, retention, legal liability, and your ability to run a sustainable program.
