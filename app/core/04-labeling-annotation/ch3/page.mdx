# Chapter 3 — Annotation Guidelines: The Engine of Consistency

Annotation quality depends on guidelines, not intelligence. The smartest annotator in the world will produce inconsistent labels if the guidelines are vague, incomplete, or untested. This chapter covers how to write, test, version, and maintain the guidelines that turn subjective human judgment into reliable, reproducible data.
