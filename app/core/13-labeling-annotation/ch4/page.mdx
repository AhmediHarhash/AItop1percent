# Chapter 4 — Workforce Design and Annotator Management

Labeling is a people operation. The quality of your labels depends on who does the labeling, how they are trained, how they are calibrated, and how they are managed over time. This chapter covers the workforce architecture that separates teams with reliable labels from teams drowning in noise.

- **4.7** — Managing Distributed and Remote Annotation Teams
