# Chapter 7 — Metric Drift, Maintenance, and Evolution

The metrics you launch with will not be the metrics you need six months later. Users change, models update, distributions shift, and what predicted quality last quarter may now predict nothing at all. Metric maintenance is not optional hygiene — it is the difference between a dashboard you trust and a dashboard you ignore.
