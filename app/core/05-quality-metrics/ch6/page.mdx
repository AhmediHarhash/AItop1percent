# Chapter 6 — Safety, Compliance, and Industry-Specific Metrics

Generic metrics tell you if your AI works. Industry-specific metrics tell you if it works in a way that keeps you in business, out of court, and trusted by the humans whose lives depend on getting it right. Every regulated domain brings its own success criteria, and if you are not measuring what auditors, patients, or legal teams care about, you are measuring the wrong thing.
