# Chapter 8 — Pricing Operations and Evolution

Pricing is not a decision you make once and forget. It is an operating system — billing infrastructure that meters usage accurately, transparency mechanisms that keep consumption-based customers from churning in frustration, competitive intelligence that tracks what alternatives cost, governance structures that prevent discount chaos from destroying your margins, and the organizational discipline to evolve pricing as your product, market, and cost structure change. The companies that treat pricing as a static number on their website lose to the companies that treat it as a dynamic, data-driven capability with its own team, its own metrics, and its own improvement cadence. This chapter builds that operational capability from billing infrastructure through pricing maturity.
