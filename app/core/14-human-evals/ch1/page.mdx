# Chapter 1 — Foundations of Human Review Infrastructure

Human review infrastructure is the invisible layer that determines whether your AI quality operations succeed or collapse under their own weight. Most teams treat review as a staffing problem—hire more people when the backlog grows, let them go when volume drops. This chapter establishes why review is fundamentally an infrastructure problem, and why the systems you build matter more than the headcount you deploy.
